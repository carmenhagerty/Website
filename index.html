What is The MyPlant Sensor?

This is a sensor for various plants that can sense water, pH, sunlight, and temperature. Using our sensor will help you better understand the health of your plant, as well as it's needs.

Our Mission

The goal of this sensor project is to create an easier way for people to keep plants alive and healthy. It can be difficult to understand why a plant is not living to its full potential, but with our sensor the process is easy and simplified.
